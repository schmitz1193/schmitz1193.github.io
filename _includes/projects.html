PROJECTS

VOLUNTEER SIGN-UP

Utilizing UI Angular Calendar, Moment, AngularJS and Firebase, a sign-up calendar for volunteers to view opportunities and get info on events they have committed to. Under special login credentials, coordinators can schedule volunteer opportunities and view who volunteered for specific events. Click on the login screen to sign-up!

TISKET/TASKET

Utilizing the MEAN Stack, created a websites for Basket Weavers to order supplies, and get feedback from other weavers on shops. The goal is to provide a creative sharing space for basket weavers to share inspiration, ideas and experiences.
